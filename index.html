Learning Christ-like Character

This is a platform where one can study and implement the characteristics of Jesus Christ into their own lives.

Learning Christ-like Character brings joy

What is Christ-like Character?

Some of the most defining character traits of Christ are faith, hope, charity and love, virtue, knowledge, patience, humility, diligence, and obedience.

Why Should Learning Christ-like Character Matter to Me?

"As we increasingly act in a manner congruent with the character of Christ, then perhaps we are indicating to heaven in a most powerful manner our desire for the supernal spiritual gift of charity." - Elder Bednar

Learning Christ-like character leads to a higher way of living; a way of living that is filled with joy.
